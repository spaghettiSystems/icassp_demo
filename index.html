Speech Emotion Recognition Demo

Background

We present samples from our model tested on the Multilingual LibriSpeech (MLS) dataset, which primarily consists of audiobook recordings. From a random selection of 100,000 audio clips from MLS, we classified each sample and identified the top 3 samples by logit value for each emotion. These demonstrations showcase our model's ability to accurately identify emotional content even in predominantly neutral speech datasets.
